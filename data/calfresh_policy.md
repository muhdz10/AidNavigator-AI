# CalFresh (California's SNAP Program)

## Program Overview

CalFresh, known federally as the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP), provides monthly food benefits to individuals and families with low-income and provides economic benefits to communities. CalFresh is the largest food program in California and provides an essential hunger safety net.

## Eligibility Requirements

### Income Eligibility
Most households must have a gross monthly income at or below 200% of the Federal Poverty Level (FPL).
- Example for a family of 3: The maximum gross monthly income is approximately $4,304.
- The net monthly income limit is 100% of the FPL.

### Work Requirements
Able-bodied adults without dependents (ABAWDs) aged 18-50 generally must work or participate in an approved employment and training program for at least 20 hours a week to receive benefits for more than 3 months in a 36-month period (though exemptions and statewide waivers often apply).

### Immigration Status
U.S. citizens and most legal permanent residents are eligible. Some noncitizens may be eligible, such as refugees, asylees, and victims of trafficking.

### Student Eligibility
College students enrolled half-time or more may be eligible if they meet certain exemptions, such as working 20 hours a week, participating in federal or state work-study, having dependents, or participating in an approved employment/training program.

## Program Benefits
Benefits are issued on an Electronic Benefit Transfer (EBT) card and can be used at grocery stores and farmers' markets to purchase most foods. The maximum monthly allotment for a family of 3 is $766 (subject to annual adjustments).

## Required Documentation
Applicants will need:
1. Proof of identity (e.g., driver's license, ID card).
2. Proof of income (e.g., recent pay stubs).
3. Proof of residency (e.g., rent receipt or utility bill).
4. Social Security numbers for all applying household members.

## How to Apply
Applications can be submitted online via BenefitsCal, in person at a county social services office, by phone, or by mail.
