# Special Supplemental Nutrition Program for Women, Infants, and Children (WIC)

## Program Overview

The Special Supplemental Nutrition Program for Women, Infants, and Children (WIC) provides federal grants to states for supplemental foods, health care referrals, and nutrition education for low-income pregnant, breastfeeding, and non-breastfeeding postpartum women, and to infants and children up to age 5 who are found to be at nutritional risk.

## Eligibility Requirements

### Categorical Eligibility
To participate, applicants must fall into one of the following categories:
- **Women**: Pregnant, postpartum (up to 6 months), or breastfeeding (up to 1 year).
- **Infants**: Up to their first birthday.
- **Children**: Up to their fifth birthday.

### Residential Eligibility
Applicants must reside in the state where they apply for WIC benefits.

### Income Eligibility
Applicants' gross income must fall at or below 185% of the U.S. Poverty Income Guidelines.
- Example for a family of 3: Maximum annual income of approximately $47,767.

### Nutritional Risk
Applicants must be seen by a health professional (e.g., physician, nurse, or nutritionist) who determines they are at nutritional risk. This could be medically based (e.g., anemia, poor pregnancy outcomes) or diet-based (e.g., inadequate dietary pattern).

## Automatic (Adjunctive) Eligibility
Certain applicants can automatically meet the income eligibility requirement if they or certain family members participate in other programs such as:
- SNAP
- Medicaid
- Temporary Assistance for Needy Families (TANF)

## Required Documentation
When applying for WIC, applicants must bring:
1. Proof of income (e.g., pay stubs, tax returns).
2. Proof of residency (e.g., utility bill, rent receipt).
3. Proof of identity (e.g., driver's license, birth certificate).
4. The individual applying for WIC must be present at the appointment.

## How to Apply
Applications are handled at the state and local levels through WIC clinics. Applicants should contact their state agency or local clinic to set up an appointment.
